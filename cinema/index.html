Cinestudio d'Or is one of the oldest movie theaters in the city of Valencia. It is located on Calle Almirante Cadarso.

Currently, it is the reference of the old cinemas in the city, since it is the last old room that is still preserved, after the closure of the Alameda Cinema, the Goya, the Swiss, the Paz, the Artis, the Tiris, the Serrano, the Capitol, the Acteón, the Aula, the Martí, the Aragón, etc. It has sessions seven days a week. The films are shown some time later than in the premiere theaters, and can be seen in double sessions for half the price of a ticket in a conventional cinema.
